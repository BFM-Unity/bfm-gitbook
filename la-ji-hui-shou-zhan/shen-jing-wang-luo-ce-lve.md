```c
激活函数

sigmoid(x) = 1/(1 + exp(−x))

tanh(x) = (exp(x) - exp(−x))/(exp(x) + exp(−x))
tanh(x) = (1 - exp(−2 * x))/(1 + exp(−2 * x))

tanh(x) = 2 * sigmoid(2 * x) - 1
tanh(x / 2) = 2 * sigmoid(x) - 1 

ReLU(x) = MAX(x,0)

Swish(x) = x * sigmoid(x)
```
